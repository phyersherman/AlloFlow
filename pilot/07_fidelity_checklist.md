## Fidelity Scoring Guide (For Research Team Use)

### Minimum Fidelity Threshold
A teacher meets "adequate fidelity" if they achieve **≥ 70%** of the following each week:
- ☐ Used AlloFlow for ≥ 3 lessons (Q1 ≥ 3)
- ☐ Adapted text to multiple reading levels (Section 2)
- ☐ Students directly interacted with AlloFlow (Section 2)
- ☐ Generated vocabulary OR quiz materials (Section 2)
- ☐ Completed this fidelity log on time

### Scoring
| Score | Criteria Met | Interpretation |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| 5/5 | All 5 criteria | High fidelity |
| 4/5 | 4 criteria | Adequate fidelity |
| 3/5 | 3 criteria | Partial fidelity |
| ≤2/5 | ≤2 criteria | Low fidelity — follow up |
