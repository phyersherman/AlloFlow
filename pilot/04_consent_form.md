## Purpose of the Study

Your child's teacher is participating in a pilot study of **AlloFlow**, a free educational software platform that helps teachers create differentiated learning materials. We are studying whether AlloFlow helps students learn more effectively and whether teachers find it useful for their instruction.

## What Will My Child Do?

If you consent, your child will:
- **Use AlloFlow** during regular classroom activities (no extra time required)
- **Complete two short surveys** (5–8 minutes each): one at the beginning and one at the end of the 4-week study period
- Activities include reading adapted texts, playing vocabulary games, answering quiz questions, and using interactive learning tools — all as part of normal classroom instruction

Your child will **not** be asked to do anything beyond their regular classroom activities. AlloFlow is simply a tool the teacher uses to create their usual lesson materials.

## What Data Will Be Collected?

| Data Type | What It Includes | Who Sees It |
|-----------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Survey responses** | Opinions about reading, learning, and technology (no right/wrong answers) | Research team only |
| **Usage data** | Which features were used, time spent (automatically logged by AlloFlow) | Research team only |
| **No PII collected** | No names, photos, or personally identifiable information | — |

**Important:** AlloFlow stores all data locally on the classroom device. **No student data is transmitted to external servers.** The platform is designed to be FERPA and COPPA compliant by architecture — there is no cloud database to breach.

## Risks and Benefits

**Risks:** This study presents **minimal risk** to your child. The activities are part of normal classroom instruction. Survey questions ask about opinions and preferences — not sensitive personal information.

**Benefits:** Your child may benefit from more engaging, appropriately-leveled learning materials. However, we cannot guarantee specific educational benefits. The broader benefit is contributing to research that may improve educational tools for all students.

## Voluntary Participation

- Participation is **completely voluntary**
- You may withdraw your child at any time without penalty
- Non-participating students will receive the same classroom instruction — they simply will not complete the surveys
- Your decision will **not** affect your child's grades or standing in any way

## Confidentiality

- All survey data will be **de-identified** (associated with a random code, not your child's name)
- Results will be reported as group averages — no individual student will be identifiable
- Data will be stored on encrypted, password-protected drives accessible only to the research team
- Data will be retained for 3 years per federal requirements, then securely destroyed
