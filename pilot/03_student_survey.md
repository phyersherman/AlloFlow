# AlloFlow Pilot — Student Survey Instrument

**Administration:** Pre-study (Week 1) and Post-study (Week 6)  
**Format:** Digital or paper — age-appropriate versions  
**Time to complete:** 5–8 minutes  
**Note:** For grades K–2, administer verbally with emoji/picture scale. For grades 3+, students can self-administer.

---
